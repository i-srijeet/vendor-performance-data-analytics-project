📊 Vendor Performance Analysis — Data Analytics Project

----

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes vendor performance, inventory efficiency, and profitability patterns to help businesses optimize pricing strategies, reduce dependency risks, and improve operational efficiency.

The analysis focuses on identifying underperforming brands, evaluating vendor contributions, and understanding how purchasing behavior impacts profitability and inventory turnover.

👉 The core business objective is to support data-driven decision-making in retail/wholesale operations.

----

🎯 Business Objectives

According to the analysis report, the project aims to:

Identify underperforming brands needing pricing or promotional changes

Determine top vendors contributing to sales and profit

Evaluate the impact of bulk purchasing on unit costs

Assess inventory turnover to reduce holding costs

Compare profitability between high- and low-performing vendors

These objectives align with improving profitability and operational efficiency.

2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis

Distribution and summary statistics

Outlier detection in pricing and freight costs

Correlation analysis between sales, margins, and turnover

3️⃣ Vendor & Brand Insights

Identified 198 brands with low sales but high margins, suggesting marketing opportunities

4️⃣ Cost & Inventory Analysis

Bulk purchasing reduces unit cost by ~72%

Slow-moving inventory identified with high holding value

5️⃣ Statistical Validation

Hypothesis testing confirmed significant differences in profit models between vendor groups

----

📈 Key Insights

Low-sales, high-margin brands can increase revenue through targeted promotions

Vendor dependency introduces supply-chain risk

Faster inventory turnover does not always mean higher profitability

Bulk purchasing significantly improves cost efficiency

These insights help businesses balance pricing, inventory, and vendor strategy.

----

💡 Recommendations

Optimize pricing for low-volume, high-margin products

Diversify vendor base to reduce risk

Use bulk purchasing strategically

Improve marketing and distribution for low-performing vendors

Reduce slow-moving inventory through demand planning
